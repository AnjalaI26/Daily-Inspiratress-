Who Will We Feature Today?

Turning back time to the 1950s, this individual worked as a mathmaticien and contributed greatly toward several NASA projects. Some of these include Freedom 7, Friendship 7, and Apollo 11–all of these required sending an astronaut into space.

Introducing... Katherine Johnson!

“Do your best, but like it. Like what you do, then you will do your best. If you don’t like what you do, shame on you.”

-NASA Interview, Sept 2017

After retiring in 1986, Johnson continued to receive awards for her work including the Presidential Medal of Freedom, and her story–along with Dorothy Vaughan and Mary Jackson who were other members of the West Computers–was featured in the 2016 film "Hidden Figures."
